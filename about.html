Get to Know Us



The Line Design Studio is a New Delhi based design and construction studio founded in 2021 by Kashif Ali. We are a passionate team of skilled architects and interior designers. Our objective is to meet the requirements and expectations of our clients through an exquisite, innovative, and unique design approach. Our team strives to overcome the limitations between design and construction while following our basic principle, that is, to deliver high-quality services and unique architectural ideas. Unbox Studio has led several projects in the field of architecture, interior, and landscape design. We focus on not just paying attention to a project’s design but also on the environmental, social and economic aspects of a project. Our team adheres to the highest professional standards throughout every stage of a project’s completion so as to build a trustable relationship with our clients.



WE BUILD FOR BETTER FUTURE

Conceptual Design

Our team prioritizes understanding the demands and expectations of our client. We prepare the conceptual design. Under this, detailed architectural designs, including details related to décor are shared for the project.

Visualization

We offer 3D visuals of our projects that enable our clients clearly visualize how the project will look post-implementation. Various detailed plans and images are also shared to strike the perfect balance between our innovation and the desire of our client.

Delivery of the Project

By obtaining final approval of our client that the requirements are being met, we then move to the practical implementation of the project. All other miscellaneous details/aspects are thoroughly discussed during this stage.
